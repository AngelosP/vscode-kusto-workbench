# You can match chart types to your question

Line and area charts are best for trends, bars are best for category comparisons, scatter charts are best for relationships, pies are best for simple proportions, and funnels are best for staged drop-off. If a chart feels noisy, it is often asking the wrong visual question. Ask Kusto Workbench agent to help you pick the right one.
