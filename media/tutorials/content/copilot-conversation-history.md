# Each query section has its own Copilot conversation

Copilot chat history is scoped to the query section where you opened it. That keeps a performance tuning conversation separate from a schema exploration conversation in another section.

![Copilot chat button for a section](images/tip-copilot-chat.png)

When the history gets stale, clear it or remove old tool calls. A smaller, cleaner conversation often produces better KQL than a long thread with outdated assumptions.
